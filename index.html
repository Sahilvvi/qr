SMS Enabled Notification Feature


SMS notification to parents regarding attendance is important now more than ever for a number of reasons.

Students are more likely to attend school if their parents know about any absences.

Studies have shown that students who are absent from school more than 10% of the time are at an increased risk of dropping out.

By sending SMS notifications to parents, schools can help to ensure that parents are aware of their child's attendance and can take steps to address any problems early on.

Advantages Of Our App

  • SMS notifications can help to improve parent-teacher communication.
